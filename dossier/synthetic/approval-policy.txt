PAYFLUX GMBH – PROCUREMENT AND APPROVAL POLICY (v2.1, effective 1 Jan 2024)

1. Every purchase of goods or services requires a purchase order (PO)
   issued in the procurement system BEFORE the vendor invoice is received.

2. Approval thresholds:
   - up to EUR 5,000: team lead approval
   - EUR 5,000 to EUR 25,000: department head approval, PO mandatory
   - above EUR 25,000: CFO approval, PO mandatory, second signature by CEO

3. Vendor bank details may only be changed upon written, signed notice by
   the vendor, verified by a call-back to a known phone number. Payments to
   bank accounts deviating from the master data record are prohibited.

4. Splitting of orders to circumvent approval thresholds is prohibited and
   will be treated as a compliance violation.
